Our Safety Promise

At FinClash, we're committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and educational environment for all users. This page outlines our community guidelines, security measures, and how to report concerns.

  • Regular security audits: We continuously monitor for vulnerabilities

  • Do not use automated tools (bots) without permission

Account Security Best Practices

Protect Your Account

  • Strong passwords: Use at least 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Unique passwords: Don't reuse passwords from other sites
  • Keep it private: Never share your password with anyone
  • Sign out: Always sign out on shared or public devices
  • Verify emails: Be cautious of phishing emails pretending to be from FinClash

Recognize Phishing Attempts

We will NEVER:

  • Ask for your password via email
  • Request sensitive information over email or chat
  • Threaten to close your account unless you provide information
  • Send unsolicited links asking you to "verify" your account

How We Respond to Violations

Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity, we may take the following actions:

  1. Warning: For minor first-time violations
  2. Temporary suspension: For repeated or moderate violations (7-30 days)
  3. Permanent ban: For severe or repeated violations
  4. Legal action: For illegal activity or threats

Appeals Process

If you believe you were suspended or banned in error:

  1. Email us at finclash101@gmail.com with subject "Account Appeal"
  2. Provide your email address and a detailed explanation
  3. We will review within 5-7 business days
  4. Our decision on appeals is final
