About Cameron

I’m Cameron, the founder of FinClash. I’ve been obsessed with markets from a young age—studying price action, earnings catalysts, and risk management while running a real portfolio and documenting what works. I noticed friends wanted to learn investing without blowing up accounts, so me and Reid built FinClash: a game where you draft tickers like a fantasy team, compete on performance, and level up your skills with clear entries, stops, and targets.

My vision is simple: make market education interactive, competitive, and honest—with data-driven tools, challenge formats, and weekly leaderboards that reward discipline over luck. Whether you’re a beginner or a stat-obsessed grinder, FinClash gives you reps, feedback, and a community that pushes you to get better.
