Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to FinClash ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our stock trading simulator platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted using bcrypt)
  • Account code (for competition access)

2.2 Trading Activity Data

We collect information about your simulated trading activity:

  • Virtual portfolio holdings and cash balance
  • Trade history (buy/sell transactions)
  • Watchlist stocks
  • Account performance metrics

2.3 Chat and Communication Data

When you use our chat features:

  • Chat messages and timestamps
  • Username associated with messages
  • Message history (up to last 1,000 messages)

2.4 Payment Information

For paid competition plans, payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card information on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy governs the use of payment information.

2.5 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns and timestamps
  • API request logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the FinClash trading simulator platform
  • Manage your account and authenticate users
  • Process simulated trades and update portfolios
  • Facilitate competitions and leaderboards
  • Enable chat functionality between users
  • Send important account notifications and updates
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Analysis and Data Processing

Our UltraThink AI feature analyzes publicly available stock market data (provided by Finnhub API) to generate trading insights. No personal information is used in AI analysis. All analysis is performed using real-time market data, historical prices, and publicly available news.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share information with:

  • Stripe - For secure payment processing
  • Finnhub API - To retrieve real-time stock market data
  • Competition Participants - Your username and performance metrics are visible to other users in competitions you join

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Password encryption using bcrypt hashing
  • HTTPS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure backend API with authentication
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited data retention (chat messages capped at 1,000)

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your trading history data

To exercise these rights, contact us at finclash101@gmail.com

8. Children's Privacy

FinClash is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use browser localStorage to maintain your login session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes. The Tailwind CDN and React libraries may use cookies for functionality purposes only.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion at any time. After deletion:

  • Your account data will be permanently removed within 30 days
  • Chat messages you sent will remain visible (with username) for continuity
  • Financial transaction records may be retained for legal compliance

11. International Users

FinClash is operated from the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and processing under U.S. laws.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or prominent notice on our platform. Your continued use of FinClash after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with:

  • Finnhub API - Stock market data (see Finnhub's privacy policy)
  • Stripe - Payment processing (see Stripe's privacy policy)
  • Socket.io - Real-time chat functionality
